# 🎵 Simple Lyrics Player + Visualizer + Aesthetic Background

A lightweight, vibe-forward lyrics player with a smooth audio visualizer and immersive backgrounds. Drop in time-stamped lyrics (LRC or plain text with timestamps), choose a background (video / GIF / WEBP / animated image), and enjoy. Double‑click the lyrics to enter **full‑screen** for peak vibes.

---

## ✨ Highlights

* **Time‑stamped lyrics playback** (LRC or timestamped text)
* **Backgrounds:** video, GIF, WEBP, and animated images supported
* **Visualizer** tied to the playing audio
* **Full‑screen lyrics:** double‑click the lyrics container
* **No account, no cloud—pure local play**
* **Responsive:** works on desktop and mobile

> ⚠️ Sync behavior: This script **does not auto‑sync** or generate timestamps. It **plays** your already time‑stamped lyrics.

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Open the player** in your browser.
2. **Load your audio file** (MP3/MP4/M4A/WAV/etc.).
3. **Add lyrics**:

   * Preferred: **.lrc** file
   * Also works: **.txt** with lines like `[mm:ss.xx] lyric line`
4. **Pick a background** (optional): video, GIF, WEBP, or animated image.
5. **Double‑click lyrics** to toggle full‑screen.

---

## 🧾 Supported Lyrics Format (Examples)

```text
[00:00.00]
[00:02.50](Oh, oh, oh, oh, oh, oh, oh)
[00:04.70](Oh, oh, oh, oh, oh, oh, oh)
[00:06.95](Oh, oh, oh)
[00:08.70]
[00:08.75]Sa sa sa save me, I don't need you to confess
[00:14.20]Got some feelings in your chest
...
```

> Any standard LRC is fine. Timestamps like `[mm:ss]` or `[mm:ss.xx]` are supported.

## 📝 FAQ

**Q: Can I use plain text lyrics without timestamps?**
A: Yes, they will display, but **won’t auto‑sync** without timestamps.

**Q: Does it support `.lrc` with offset tags?**
A: Standard `[mm:ss]` and `[mm:ss.xx]` timestamps work great. Offsets may be ignored depending on implementation.

**Q: What media can I use for backgrounds?**
A: Video (e.g., MP4/WebM), GIF, WEBP, and animated images.
